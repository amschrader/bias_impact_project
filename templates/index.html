Gender Bias Simulator
  • OPEN SOURCE

THE CONCEPT

What is the impact of gender bias?

Can a small amount of bias during job promotions have a measurable impact on a company's hierarchy?

This simplified simulation was inspired by "Male-Female Differences: A Computer Simulation"

BASIS FOR THIS SIMULATION

* Each employee level begins with equal number of men and women.

* At each promotional period 15% of employees at all levels are randomly removed. (15% attrition rate)

* 12 promotional cycles (2 per year for 6 years)

* Promotion scores are randomly assigned from 1-100, or 1-100 + bias amount for the 'favored' gender.
